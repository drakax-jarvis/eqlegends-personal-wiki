Game at a Glance

🎵 Triple-Class System

Choose up to 3 classes per character — all abilities active simultaneously. 560 possible combinations from 16 classes.

⚔️ Stance-Based Combat

Classic disciplines replaced by 5 stances: Balanced, Offensive, Defensive, Striker, and Berserker.

🎯 Gear Upgrade +10

Upgrade items to +10 via merging duplicates or Motes. Exaltate focus effects, procs, and appearances.

🏰 Classic Norrath

Pre-Kunark: Antonica, Faydwer, Odus. All original races (15 total), classic art, music, zones, and loot.

🎮 Solo to Raid

Solo-friendly design. Groups up to 4, raids up to 8. Instanced zones. No corpse runs. No XP loss on death.

🔄 No Bard Twisting

Songs activate once and sustain automatically. No more song twisting. Instrument focus items are all/all.
