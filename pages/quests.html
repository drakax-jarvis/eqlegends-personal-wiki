Quest Walkthroughs

Step-by-step guides with time estimates for 16 essential quests. Data from eqlwiki.com.

Shared — Both Combos

1. Journeyman's Boots Confirmed

Start: Hasten Bootstrutter (wandering gnome, Rathe Mountains) | Min Lvl: 32+ solo | Time: 4-20h

Reward: Click-to-cast Spirit of Wolf run speed — #1 most important item in the game.

☐ Step 1: Kill Ancient Cyclops for Ring of the Ancients. Camp in Ocean of Tears (island 8) or Southern Desert of Ro (10% night spawn). Rarest part.

☐ Step 2: Kill "a shadowed man" for Shadowed Rapier. NO RENT — must be turned in within 30 min of looting.

☐ Step 3: Give Hasten Bootstrutter: Ring of the Ancients + Shadowed Rapier + 3250 gold (NOT platinum). Get Journeyman's Boots.

2. Mystic Cloak Confirmed

Start: Tolkar Parlone, N. Felwithe | Min Lvl: 30+ | Class: NEC/WIZ/MAG/ENC | Time: 2-4h

Reward: Back, AC 8, INT+5, SV MAGIC+5. Effect: Steelskin (10 charges, inventory-clickable).

☐ Step 1: Hail Tolkar, ask "Who is your daughter?" then "Where is she adventuring?"

☐ Step 2: Kill Priest of Najena (Unrest, 3rd floor SW tower) → loot Tarnished Bronze Key.

☐ Step 3: Go to Najena. Get Guard's Keyring (kill ogres or Moosh).

☐ Step 4: Give Tarnished Bronze Key to Linara Parlone (Najena jail cell) → get A Sealed Letter.

☐ Step 5: Return letter to Tolkar → get Faded Cloak.

☐ Step 6: Give Faded Cloak to Trilani Parlone (Erudin Enchanter Guild) → get Mystic Cloak.

3. Stein of Moggok Confirmed

Start: Clurg (bartender, Oggok) | Min Lvl: 4+ | Time: 3-8h

Reward: Primary/Sec, DEX+5, INT+10, HP+10, SV DIS+25, light source. Effect: Light Healing.

☐ Step 1: Get Kiola Nut — Cleonae Kalen, Ocean of Tears (Sisters Island). Use Wood Elf illusion.

☐ Step 2: Get Honey Jum — 3 Honeycombs + 30gp to Kizzie Mintopp, Rivervale. Use Gnome illusion.

☐ Step 3: Get Erud's Tonic — Innoruuk's Kiss to Sinnkin Highbrow, Erudin Library 3F.

☐ Step 4: Get Koalindl Fish — Kill Frostbite (W. Karana) or fish in Qeynos aqueducts.

☐ Step 5: Give all 4 items to Gregor Nasin (Freeport) → get Barkeep Compendium.

☐ Step 6: Give Compendium to Clurg (Oggok) → get Stein of Moggok.

4. Aegis of Life Confirmed

Start: Capt Linarius, N. Karana (bridge to S. Karana) | Min Lvl: 35+ | Class: CLR/PAL | Time: 4-10h

Reward: Back/Secondary, AC 12, WIS+10, SV DIS+25. Effect: Superior Healing (1 charge, equip click).

⚠️ FACTION: Must be WARMLY with Camlend Serbold (N. Qeynos priest guild). Raise via Lashun Novashine (Qeynos newbie yard, 2g at a time).

☐ Step 1: Hail Capt Linarius, ask about "local dangers" then "Lord Grimrot".

☐ Step 2: Go to S. Karana, undead camp near bridge. Kill putrid skeleton standing (placeholder). Respawn: 3h 12min.

☐ Step 3: Kill Lord Grimrot (live) → loot Pestilence Scythe (one with the proc).

☐ Step 4: Kill Lord Grimrot (undead) → loot Decaying Heart.

☐ Step 5: Turn in BOTH items to Camlend Serbold simultaneously → get Aegis of Life.

5. Screaming Mace Confirmed

Start: Dwarven smith + elven priest, Crushbone | Min Lvl: 18+ | Time: 2-4h

Reward: 1HB, DMG 8, DLY 35. Proc: STR+AC buff at L10. All classes except NEC/WIZ/MAG/ENC.

☐ Step 1: Kill Emperor Crush → loot Dwarven Ringmail Tunic. Give to dwarven smith → get Dwarven Mace.

☐ Step 2: Kill orc warlord → loot Bracers of Battle. Give to elven priest (say "The king is dead" first) → get Prayer Cloth of Tunare.

☐ Step 3: Give Dwarven Mace + Prayer Cloth to elven priest → get Screaming Mace.

6. Hero Bracers Confirmed

Start: Grahan Rothkar, S. Qeynos arena basement | Min Lvl: 30+ | Time: 2-6h

Reward: Wrist, AC 9, STR+10, SV DIS+5, SV POI+5.

☐ Step 1 (optional): Go to Erud's Crossing (Wisp Isle). Kill Nomala (catman by fire, 1-2h respawn) → loot Pen Key. Return to Grahan.

☐ Step 2: Go to Steamfont Mountains minotaur cave. Kill minotaur slavers → spawns Minotaur Hero (RNG).

☐ Step 3: Kill Minotaur Hero → loot Minotaur Hero Shackles. Return to Grahan → get Hero Bracers.

Dave's Combo — Priority Quests

7. Ghoulbane Confirmed

Start: Ryshon Hunsti, W. Karana | OR ask Ruathey directly | Min Lvl: 30 | Class: PAL | Time: 2-4h

Reward: 1H Slash, DMG 15, DLY 34. Proc: Dismiss Undead at L20. MAGIC LORE.

☐ Step 1: Find Ruathey (Lavastorm, -1512,275 near Nektulos zoneline). Ask "What bag" → get Nobleman's Bag. (Skip the optional note-running.)

☐ Step 2: Farm Amstaf's Scroll — goblins in High Keep basement.

☐ Step 3: Farm Blade of Nobility — "a crazed goblin" in Butcherblock.

☐ Step 4: Farm Ghoul's Heart — ghouls in Estate of Unrest.

☐ Step 5: Farm Hilt of the Nobleman — skeletons in Najena.

☐ Step 6: Combine all 4 items + Scroll in Nobleman's Bag → Ghoulbane.

8. SoulFire Confirmed

Start: Kalatrina Plossen, N. Freeport | Min Lvl: 50 | Time: 10-20h

Reward: Paladin sword with undead slaying + healing capabilities.

☐ Part A: Brew Bog Juice + Edible Goo. Buy 4× Drom's Champagne → Tykar Renlin (E. Freeport) → get Bunker Cell. Turn in Bog Juice + Goo + Cell to prisoner (W. Freeport arena) → get H.K. 102. Give to Bank Clerk Jaylin (Highkeep) → get A Sealed Note.

☐ Part B: Get Spider Venom Sac + Cloth Shirt. Give shirt to Altunic Jartin (EC) → Token of Generosity. Give Venom Sac to Merko Quetalis (N. Freeport) → Token of Bravery. Give both tokens to Merko → spawns Guard Willia (Freeport sewers). Kill fast → loot Token of Truth. Give to Merko → get Testimony.

☐ Part C: Kill Xicotl (rare spawn, Mistmoore pool room, ~L43 SK) → loot Glowing Sword Hilt (100%).

☐ Part D: Kill Sir Lucan D'Lere (W. Freeport, 7h spawn) → loot Testimony of Truth. Requires warmly+ Knights of Truth faction. Give to Valeron Dushire → get Brilliant Sword of Faith.

☐ Part E: Requires warmly+ Priests of Life faction (Bone Chips in Qeynos). Go to Splitpaw Lair jail cells, find Brother Hayle. Give him Sealed Note (he opens it), then give opened note + Testimony + Glowing Sword Hilt + Brilliant Sword of Faith all together → SOULFIRE.

9. Fiery Avenger Confirmed

Start: Inte Akera, Plane of Sky | Min Lvl: 46 | Prereq: Ghoulbane + SoulFire | Time: 20-40h

Note: Requires Plane of Sky access, boss dragon kills (Nagafen/Vox), and Miragul kill (L53). Requires warmly+ Deepwater Knights faction.

☐ Step 1: Get Ghoulbane and SoulFire (previous quests).

☐ Step 2: Get Torn burnt book (Nagafen/Talendor/Severilous) + Torn frost book (Vox/Gorenaire/Faydedar).

☐ Step 3: Give 1000pp + both books to Rysva To'Biath (Neriak) or Rineval Talyas (Qeynos) → get Book of Scale.

☐ Step 4: Give Book of Scale to Oracle of K'Arnon → get Miragul's Phylactery.

☐ Step 5: Charm Lich of Miragul (Everfrost ice caves), turn in Phylactery → spawns Miragul (L53 necro). Kill with group/raid → loot Miragul's Robe + Head.

☐ Step 6: In Plane of Sky: give SoulFire to Inte Akera alone → get Blessing of Nobility. Give Ghoulbane alone → get Blessing of Sacrifice. Then give head + robe + both blessings together → Fiery Avenger.

10. Chalice of Conquest Confirmed

Start: Dru Razbind, Butcherblock | Min Lvl: 35 | Time: 4-8h

Reward: Holy Partisan of Underfoot (2H piercer). Requires amiable+ Paladins of Underfoot faction.

☐ Step 1: Talk to Dru Razbind → get Chalice case - empty.

☐ Step 2: Talk to Nicholas (Rathe Mtns) → get Stem of Candlestick + Klunga's Bracelet.

☐ Step 3: Kill Unkempt Druids (Rathe Mtns) → loot Foot of Candlestick (rare).

☐ Step 4: Loot Abandoned Orc Shovel — ground spawn at +3486, -663 in Lake Rathetear orc camp.

☐ Step 5: Get Soil of Underfoot — 4 Fairy Dust to Priestess Ghalea (N. Kaladim).

☐ Step 6: Get Honeycomb from bees anywhere.

☐ Step 7: Give Stem + Foot + Honeycomb + Soil to Dok (Halas) → get Candle of Bravery.

☐ Step 8: Kill Exiled Legionnaire (Dagnor's Cauldron orc camp) → loot Blood Stained Note. Give note + Klunga's Bracelet + 100gp to Ghilanbiddle (Fabulous Four camp, Dagnor's).

☐ Step 9: Give shovel to Captain Klunga (L22 orc, Dagnor's) → follows you, digs up bag, goes aggro. Kill him → loot Chalice of Conquest.

☐ Step 10: Combine Chalice + Candle in Chalice case → filled case. Give to Datur Nightseer (N. Kaladim) → get Cape of Underfoot. Give Cape to Dru Razbind → get Holy Partisan of Underfoot.

11. Rod of Insidious Glamour Confirmed

Start: Sultin, Temple of Solusek Ro (lower library, west wing) | Min Lvl: 25 | Class: ENC | Time: 1-3h

Reward: 1HB, DMG 6, AC 2, CHA+12. Effect: Alliance (instant cast, GCD reset).

⚠️ FACTION: Must be amiable+ with Temple of Sol Ro before final turn-in. Cast Benevolence on Sultin or donate to Rathmana Allin (S. Desert of Ro, 30gp).

☐ Step 1: Kill froglok priest (Upper Guk, same room as summoner) → loot Golden Rod.

☐ Step 2: Kill "a shadowed man" (various zones) → loot A Shining Stone (NOT no-rent).

☐ Step 3: Give Shining Stone + 50gp to Cynthia (Rathe Mtns gypsy camp) → get Glamour Stone.

☐ Step 4: Give Glamour Stone + 50gp to Tarn Visilin (High Keep 1F) → get Glowing Glamour Stone.

☐ Step 5: Give Golden Rod + Glowing Glamour Stone to Sultin → get Rod of Insidious Glamour.

Christy's Combo — Priority Quests

12. Paw of Opolla Confirmed

Start: Vin Moltor, Qeynos Catacombs | Min Lvl: 29 solo | Class: CLR | Time: 12-45h (Gynok Moltor is the bottleneck at 10-40h)

Reward: Primary/Sec, AC 9, STA+5, WIS+9, AGI+9, MANA+5.

☐ Step 1: Get Severed Paw — either bake a Rat Ear Pie (Baking 135+) for Vin Moltor, or just kill Vin (25% drop, ~1h camp).

☐ Step 2: Camp Gynok Moltor (Befallen entrance, L24-25, extremely rare 0.5-2% on 16min PH) → loot Platinum Ring. This is the bottleneck.

☐ Step 3-5: Kill Fungus Mutant (Upper Guk, 16:30 respawn) for Degenerated Guk Weed + Faerix Spores + Guk Bracket Mildew. Trade to Melixis (Kerra Isle) for Gold, Silver, and Copper Rings.

☐ Step 6: Combine all 4 rings in Severed Paw → Paw of Opolla.

13. Staff of Temperate Flux Confirmed

Start: Gardern, Temple of Solusek Ro (balcony, +425,-70) | Min Lvl: 30-35 solo | Class: WIZ | Time: 3-8h

Reward: 1HB, DMG 6, DLY 30, SV FIRE+5, SV COLD+5. Effect: LowerElement I (instant cast at L20).

☐ Step 1: Get Darkwood Staff — give Romar Sunto (Temple) a Lambent Stone.

☐ Step 2: Kill inferno goblin wizard (Solusek A) → Heart of Fire.

☐ Step 3: Kill goblin wizard (Permafrost) → Heart of Frost.

☐ Step 4: Kill stone skeleton (Lake Rathetear, SE tower, L24-26) → Rod of Bone. Hardest part — earth pets root.

☐ Step 5: Give all 4 items to Gardern (hail, "What lore to the staff?", "I am interested") → get Staff of Temperate Flux.

14. Necromancer Skullcap Beta Needed

Start: Master Xydoz, W. Cabilis Tower of Death | Min Lvl: 4+ | Class: NEC | Time: Dozens of hours (Kunark expansion)

⚠️ NOTE: Most components are Kunark-era and tagged OUT OF ERA — this entire chain may not be available at classic launch. Included for reference once Kunark releases. Ranks 1-7 require Kunark zones (Lake of Ill Omen, Field of Bone, Warsliks Woods, etc.) and tradeskills (Tailoring, Blacksmithing, Brewing).

15. Thex Mallet Confirmed

Start: Loveal S'Nez, Neriak Third Gate | Min Lvl: 20+ | Class: NEC/SHD | Time: 2-4h

Reward: Range/Primary/Sec, 1HB, DMG 5, DLY 33, SV MAGIC+5. Also get Reaper of the Dead.

⚠️ FACTION: Must be amiable+ with The Dead (Neriak necro guild). Very hard for non-Dark-Elves. Use DE form or Guise of the Deceiver.

☐ Step 1: Hail Loveal S'Nez, say "I am interested in your appointed tasks."

☐ Step 2: Find Kizdean Gix (W. Commonlands tollbooth). Say password: "Utalk Adarev Otcin" → get A Tattered Cloth Note.

☐ Step 3: Give note to Skeleton Lrodd (Befallen, by the well, indifferent) → spawns Cmdr Windstream on level 3.

☐ Step 4: Kill Cmdr Windstream → loot Mallet Stem.

☐ Step 5: Kill An Elf Skeleton (same floor, beyond locked door, 6:40 respawn) → loot Mallet Head + Hilt.

☐ Step 6: Combine Head + Hilt in Stem → Thex Mallet.

☐ Step 7: Return Thex Mallet to Loveal S'Nez → get Reaper of the Dead.

16. Caduceus of Sacrament Confirmed

Start: Gavel the Temperant, Temple of Solusek Ro | Min Lvl: 1 (realistically 20+) | Class: CLR | Time: 1-3h

Reward: Primary, 1HB, DMG 12, DLY 32. MAGIC LORE NO DROP.

☐ Step 1: Get 2× Magnetized Platinum Bars — give enchanted platinum to Ostorm (NPC, Temple of Sol Ro).

☐ Step 2: Get Icon of Sacrament — ground spawn in Mistmoore Castle jail at (-68, 280).

☐ Step 3: Get Ingot of Sacrament — kill Giant Skeleton (Rathe Mountains ruins, -600, -1600). Darker than normal skeletons.

☐ Step 4: Give all items to Gavel the Temperant → get Caduceus of Sacrament.
