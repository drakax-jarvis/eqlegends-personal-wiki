Shadow Knight

The dark-armored paragon — fear, lifetaps, disease, and death.

☠️ Shadow Knight

Role: Tank / DPS / Debuffer / Puller

Plate Armor2H SlashTankFeign Death

Core Description

Shadow Knights are the dark mirror of Paladins — unholy warriors who blend heavy plate armor with necromantic magic and disease-based attacks. They are exceptional tanks capable of generating tremendous hate through damage and fear effects, and their ability to Feign Death makes them superb pullers in dangerous dungeons.

Shadow Knights are defined by their lifetap spells, which simultaneously damage enemies and heal the SK. They wield disease and poison-based magic, can instill fear in enemies, and possess the iconic Harm Touch — a devastating single-target nuke on a long cooldown. Their Feign Death ability allows them to fake death, dropping aggro and enabling safe pulling in group content.

☠️ EQL Shadow Knight Change

In EQL, Shadow Knights are a full plate tank with all the survival tools they need to main tank. Lifetaps generate significantly more aggro on EQL and are more mana-efficient than classic. Harm Touch has a reduced cooldown and auto-scales with level. Shadow Knights also gain Gate at L1, giving them unprecedented utility for a tank class right from the start. The disease spell line sees major improvements — disease counters are far more common on EQL mobs and several spells have been given increased range and reduced cast times.

Stat Priorities

  1. Strength (STR): Melee damage, carry capacity for heavy armor. Shadow Knights need STR for threat generation through melee.
  2. Stamina (STA): HP pool. As a main tank, survivability is critical and STA directly increases hit points.
  3. Intelligence (INT): Mana pool. SK spells are mana-based and INT determines how much mana you have for lifetaps, fear, and disease spells.
  4. Dexterity (DEX): Affects weapon procs. Lowest priority since SK relies more on spells for threat than auto-attack procs.

EQL-Specific Shadow Knight Changes

  • Gate at L1 — Shadow Knights start with Gate, giving them unprecedented mobility for a tank class
  • Harm Touch auto-scales — damage increases with level, no need to memblur and re-memorize
  • Harm Touch reduced cooldown — shorter reuse timer than classic EQ
  • Feign Death at ~L14 — SKs get FD later than Monks but earlier than classic, enhancing pulling utility
  • Lifetaps generate more aggro — directly increases threat, making SK tanking very competitive with Warriors and Paladins
  • Improved disease spells — disease counters are more common on EQL mobs, and disease DoTs have been buffed in both damage and duration
  • All/all instruments — instrument focus items work for SK too in EQL
  • Sneak — available to Shadow Knight (shared with Bard, Monk, Ranger, Rogue)
  • Boon of the Guru — (werewolf illusion + lifetap + dex) is a spell available to Shadow Knights in EQL

Shadow Knight AA Priority

  1. Harm Touch Mastery — reduces Harm Touch cooldown and increases damage
  2. Fear Mastery — improved fear duration and resist rate
  3. Lifetap Efficiency — reduces mana cost of lifetap spells
  4. Disease Specialty — increases disease DoT damage and duration
  5. Plate Armor Proficiency — increases AC from worn armor
  6. Feign Death Mastery — reduces FD failure chance and reuse timer
