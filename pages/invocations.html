Invocation System — Caster Stances

EQL's spellcaster stance system — active modes that alter how your spells behave. Separate from melee stances; both can be active simultaneously.

What Are Invocations?

Invocations are the caster equivalent of melee stances. They are active modes that modify the behavior, cost, and effect of your spells. Per the EQL Wiki patch notes: "Invocations are in and can be used in tandem with Melee Stances." This means you can have a melee stance (Balanced / Offensive / Defensive) and an invocation active at the same time.

There are 7 invocations confirmed in the EQL Wiki patch notes. Different classes have access to different invocations — hybrids (Paladin, Bard, Beastlord, Ranger, SK) get a subset, while pure casters (Cleric, Necromancer, Wizard, Enchanter, Magician, Druid, Shaman) may get different options.

How Invocations Work

  • Invocations are separate from melee stances. You can have a melee stance (Balanced/Offensive/Defensive) AND an invocation active simultaneously.
  • Only one invocation can be active at a time.
  • Invocations have endurance costs while active (varies by invocation).
  • The Strategy skill improves stance/invocation efficiency — train it early.
  • Invocations are activated via the invocation UI or /alt act [number] macros.

⭐ Recommended Default for Each Combo

Dave: Defensive Stance + Spellblade (Strike) = tank + spell-proc DPS. This is your primary combat mode.

Christy: Balanced Stance + Recovery = maximum mana efficiency. Swap to Overchannel for bosses, Divine/Inviolable for emergencies, Empowering for healing phases.

Key Interactions

  • Spellblade + Defensive Stance: Reduces incoming damage by 50% while your melee swings proc bonus magic damage. This is the core of Dave's tank-DPS hybrid playstyle.
  • Divine + Cleric healing: Every Cleric heal spell also heals the lowest HP group member again. With all the WIS classes in the Christy's combo, this doubles down.
  • Overchannel + Tashan: Dave casts Tashan (resist debuff) + Enchanter's natural bonuses + Overchannel's -115/-145 resist = spells practically cannot be resisted.
  • Recovery + Bard Clarity song + Enchanter Clarity spell: These three stack for extreme mana regen. The duo will have nearly infinite mana.
