Gear Upgrade System

How to upgrade items to +10 and use Exaltations.

Item Upgrading (+0 to +10)

EQL introduces a gear upgrade system. Items can be upgraded from +0 to +10 through two methods:

Method 1: Merging Duplicates

Combine two copies of the same item to increase its + value. A +0 and +0 combine to make +1. A +1 and +1 make +2. This continues up to +10.

Method 2: Motes

Motes are a tiered drop currency that can be used as upgrade material in place of duplicate items. Different tiers of Motes exist for different item levels/ranges. Motes are found as normal drops from mobs throughout Norrath.

💡 Upgrade Strategy

Prioritize upgrading: Weapon → Chest → Shield → Helm → Rings → Rest. Each +1 adds stats and effectiveness. A +10 weapon is dramatically stronger than its base version.

Exaltations

Exaltations replace the classic EQ vendor augment system. They allow you to:

  • Merge focus effects between items (e.g., take the heal focus from a robe and merge it to your chest)
  • Merge proc effects (e.g., move a weapon proc to a different slot)
  • Merge appearances (transmog — change how your gear looks)

Haste is NOT an Exaltation — it remains a separate stat on items.

Stat Caps

  • Stats currently cap at 510
  • Mix of hard caps, soft caps, and uncapped stats
  • Haste cap: An upgraded 41% item + clicky/proc reaches cap. External haste buffs are less critical for geared characters.

Item Trading Rules

  • Planar gear is no-trade
  • Pre-planar gear (like Cloaked Flames) is tradable
  • No level requirements on gear currently planned
  • Recommended levels under consideration (player-friendly design)

Motes Farming Tips

  • Higher level zones drop higher tier Motes
  • Dungeon crawl mode offers bonus Mote rewards
  • Group up for faster farming — 4-player groups clear faster
  • Named mobs drop better Mote tiers
