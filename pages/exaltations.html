Exaltations Guide

How to transfer focus effects, procs, clicks, and appearances between items. Data from eqlwiki.com.

What Are Exaltations?

Exaltations replace the classic EQ augment system. They let you take special properties (focus effects, procs, clicky effects, worn effects, even visual appearance) from one item and transfer them to another. A fully upgraded item (+4) has all 5 slot types available.

The 5 Exaltation Slot Types

Slot TypeItem LevelWhat It Does
Ornamentation+0 (base)Changes item appearance to another item's look. Uses Ornamentation Tokens (not yet available in beta).
Focus+1Transfers a Focus Effect (e.g., Improved Healing, Burning Affliction) from another item.
Click+2Transfers a Click Effect (activated ability) from another item.
Worn+3Transfers a passive Worn Effect (e.g., mana regen, stat bonus while equipped) from another item.
Proc+4Transfers a Combat Proc (e.g., lifetap, stun, DD on hit) from another item.

How It Works

  1. Level up a source item to the required + level (merge duplicates or use Motes).
  2. At the appropriate + level, the effect becomes a removable Exaltation — click the slot to extract it.
  3. Insert the Exaltation into the matching slot on your target item.
  4. The source item loses the effect — but you can always move it back later.

Restrictions

  • Class restrictions: An Exaltation can only go into an item if there is class overlap between the two. Once inserted, the item's usable classes become the intersection of both.
  • Slot restrictions: A 2H weapon Exaltation is Primary-only. A 1H weapon Exaltation can go in Primary or Secondary.
  • Haste cannot be Exaltated — it stays as a native item stat.

💡 Exaltation Tips

  • Focus effects and procs from pre-planar gear are tradable — buy duplicates cheaply to level up and extract the effect.
  • Planar gear is no-trade — you must farm duplicates yourself to upgrade.
  • You can move Exaltations freely between items — experiment with different combinations.
  • Haste cannot be Exaltated — it stays native on items. Don't waste effort trying to move it.
  • With +4 on a weapon, you unlock Ornamentation (appearance) + Focus + Click + Worn + Proc simultaneously.
