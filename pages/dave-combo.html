Bard + Paladin + Enchanter

Dave's Hybrid Tank / Support / Crowd Control Trio

Bard Paladin Enchanter

🎯 Combo Rating

This is an S-Tier solo / duo combo that covers every essential role: tanking (Paladin plate + defensive stance), healing (Paladin heals + Lay on Hands), crowd control (Enchanter mez + charm + pacify), and utility (Bard songs for run speed, mana regen, attack speed, resists, tracking). Extremely strong for the open beta.

Why This Combo Works

The Bard/Paladin/Enchanter combo is a Swiss Army knife that can handle any situation:

  • Paladin provides plate armor, Defensive stance (50% damage reduction), heals, stuns, Lay on Hands, and undead smiting. Your primary tank and off-healer.
  • Bard provides permanent passive songs: run speed (Selo's), mana regen (Clarity-equivalent), attack speed (Haste), damage shields, resists, and tracking. No twisting needed — activate and forget.
  • Enchanter provides crowd control (mezmerize, pacify, charm), mana regen (Clarity), illusion spells, Haste, and Slow. The charm AA makes charm 100% unbreakable.

💡 Recommended Starting Race

Bard primary → Half Elf. Gives you 90 CHA (excellent for Bard songs and Enchanter charm tick checks), 90 AGI for defense, 85 DEX for instrument fizzle reduction. Paladin secondary (all races can be Paladin secondary). Enchanter third at level 10.

Stat Priority

  1. Charisma (CHA): Affects Bard lull songs AND Enchanter charm tick checks. Both classes want this high.
  2. Wisdom (WIS): Increases Paladin mana pool for heals, stuns, and buffs.
  3. Stamina (STA): Increases HP. Paladin is your tank — you need HP.
  4. Dexterity (DEX): Reduces Bard song fizzle chance, affects weapon procs.
  5. Intelligence (INT): Increases Enchanter mana pool for Mesmerize, Tash, Clarity, Haste.
  6. Strength (STR): Carry capacity and melee damage.

Playstyle Rotation

  1. Pre-fight: Cast buffs (Clarity, Valor, Selo's, Anthem, Psalm). Keep songs active.
  2. Pull: Use Enchanter pacify or Bard lull to split packs. Selo's + snare kiting if needed.
  3. Engage: Defensive stance. Paladin stun for initial aggro. Mez adds immediately.
  4. Sustain: Clarity song + Clarity spell stack for maximum mana regen. Bard passive HP/MP regen ticking.
  5. Charm: When safe, charm a strong mob for bonus DPS. Enchanter AA makes charm 100% unbreakable.
  6. Emergency: Color Flux AE stun + Lay on Hands if HP drops. Christy (Cleric) can assist with heals.
  7. After fight: Bard mana regen + Paladin self-heals = zero downtime between pulls.

Gearing Philosophy

Since you wear plate armor (from Paladin), prioritize finding gear that boosts these stats:

  • CHA items for Bard songs and Enchanter charms
  • WIS items for Paladin mana pool
  • HP/AC — you're the tank. Raw survivability first.
  • Instrument modifiers for Bard songs (all/all items in EQL — any slot)
  • Two "any" slots can hold a shield (full AC) and a breastplate (stat stacking)

Synergies with Christy's Combo

This combo pairs extremely well with Christy (Cleric)/Necromancer/Wizard:

  • Your Bard Clarity song + Enchanter Clarity spell stack, giving Christy's caster trio immense mana regen
  • Your Paladin tanking lets Christy (Necromancer) and Wizard nuke freely without aggro
  • Christy (Cleric) can main-heal while you off-heal and tank
  • Her Wizard + your Paladin stuns = massive burst damage
  • Her Necro roots/snares + your Enchanter mezzes = ultimate battlefield control
  • Her Cleric resurrections + your Paladin resurrection = safety net
