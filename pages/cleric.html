🙏 Cleric

Role: Healer / Buffer / Resurrector

Plate Armor1H BluntHealerBuffer

Core Description

Clerics are the undisputed masters of healing in EverQuest. They wear plate armor for survivability and channel divine magic to heal wounds, cure ailments, and protect allies from harm. No other class comes close to the Cleric's raw healing throughput.

EQL-Specific Cleric Changes

🆕 Cleric — Most New Spells of Any Class

Cleric received the most new spells of any class in EQL. Promise Renewal was reworked June 29: 1 second cast time, increased mana cost, heals for 5000 (was 0.25s instant). Duration Heals (Celestial Remedy/Health/Healing) had mana costs reduced. New heal lines including fast cast heals and a Promised healing line with HoT components. Clerics remain the unquestioned best healers in the game.

Stat Priorities

  1. Wisdom (WIS): Mana pool. The Cleric's most important stat.
  2. Stamina (STA): HP. Plate-wearing clerics can take hits.
  3. Dexterity (DEX): Reduces spell fizzle.
  4. Intelligence (INT): Less important — Cleric casts from WIS pool.

Why Cleric in Christy's Combo?

Cleric provides the healing backbone for the duo. Her 96% rezzes + your Paladin's rezzes = safety net. Cleric plate + Promise Renewal makes the entire combo much more durable. Meanwhile, Necromancer DoTs and Wizard nukes provide all the damage you need.
