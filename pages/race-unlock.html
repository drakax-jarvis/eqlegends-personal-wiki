Race Unlock Guide — Dark Elf Priority

How to unlock Dark Elf and other races via faction achievements. Data from Alanna's Race Unlock Guide on eqlwiki.com.

🎯 Your Goal

Both characters want to switch to Dark Elf as soon as possible. Dark Elf gives Ultravision (see in darkness), Hide (sneak while standing still), and excellent hybrid stats: STR 60, STA 65, AGI 90, DEX 75, WIS 83, INT 99, CHA 60 — the best INT for Enchanter/Necro/Wizard while still having solid WIS for Cleric/Paladin.

How Race Unlocking Works

Races are unlocked via achievements in the "Races" subsection of "Untapped Potential" (EverQuest > Progression menu).

  • Each unlockable race needs +2000 personal faction with 3 specific factions
  • Faction starts at 0 and goes to +2000 — no negatives
  • Minimum +5 faction per action guaranteed
  • Race Unlock Tokens (FAQ mentions L50 reward) are not yet implemented — faction grinding is the only method
  • Starting as a race auto-grants its achievement
  • Change race via the Loadout system once unlocked

Dark Elf Unlock — Step by Step

Achievement: "Race Unlock - Dark Elf"

Required Factions (+2000 each)

Method: Bottle of Red Wine (Cheap, Recommended)

  1. Buy 400 Bottles of Red Wine — cheap from any vendor (Swin Blackeye in East Freeport, or Neriak merchants)
  2. Travel to Neriak Third Gate
  3. Find Lokar To'Biath. If KoS, use Sneak (behind him), Feign Death, or Invisibility (cast invis after opening trade window)
  4. Trade wines 4 at a time. Each gives: Dreadguard Inner +5, Dreadguard Outer +5, Dark Bargainers +10
  5. 400 wines = all 3 factions capped. Dark Bargainers caps at 200
  6. Achievement unlocks — change to Dark Elf via Loadout UI

💡 Faction Tricks for Non-Dark Elves

Enchanter Alliance spell (+100 temp faction), Bard faction song, or simply Sneak behind Lokar — he'll con Indifferent while you trade. Cost is only a few platinum for the wine.
