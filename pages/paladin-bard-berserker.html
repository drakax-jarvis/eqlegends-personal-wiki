Jessy's Combo — Paladin / Bard / Berserker

The holy war machine — unkillable plate tank, relentless axe DPS, and full Bard utility in one character.

🎯 Combo Rating: A-Tier Melee Hybrid

Three plate classes in one character — Paladin (tank + heals + stuns), Bard (songs + pull + utility), Berserker (axe DPS + Frenzy burst). No mana dependency for primary damage. Self-sufficient with healing, tanking, DPS, and utility all rolled into one. Great in a duo with another melee or with Christy's caster combo.

How the Combo Works

A fully self-sufficient melee powerhouse. Every class wears plate, every class does something useful in every fight.

  • Paladin — Main tank + healer. Plate armor, stuns, self-heals, Lay on Hands (full heal), rez. Smite (new EQL skill: 3.5x damage, reduces LoH cooldown). Spellblade Invocation makes your first spell gem proc on melee swings — ideal with Stun or Strike loaded.
  • Bard — Permanent buffs. Selo's run speed, Clarity mana regen for Paladin spells, haste songs for Berserker DPS. Pull utility with lull and pacify. Background Aura AA lets you maintain a song while casting other spells.
  • Berserker — Primary DPS. Axe specialization, Frenzy (burst damage), War Cry (group buff), throwing axes for pulling/range. Attacks scale with STR. No mana dependency — all endurance-based.

Stat Priorities

  1. Strength (STR): Melee damage for Berserker + Paladin. Bard songs don't scale with STR, but you melee with all three classes.
  2. Stamina (STA): HP for the tank who stands in front. All three classes benefit.
  3. Wisdom (WIS): Paladin mana pool for heals and stuns. Only the Paladin uses mana.
  4. Charisma (CHA): Bard song effectiveness + lull/charm tick checks. Lower priority since Bard songs sustain automatically in EQL.
  5. Dexterity (DEX): Weapon procs for Berserker axes and Paladin swords. Reduces spell fizzle.

Spellblade Strategy

As a Paladin, you have access to Spellblade Invocation — your first spell gem fires as a proc on melee swings. This is a cornerstone of your DPS.

  • Default: Load Strike (Paladin DD, L15) as your first gem. Every melee swing adds magic damage.
  • Undead: Load Instrument of Knives (L24) — undead-only DD proc, excellent in Befallen and Plane of Fear.
  • Stun lock: Load Stun (L1) for caster mobs — every swing interrupts casting.

Duo & Group Strategy

Duo with a Healer (Cleric or Druid)

This combo is exceptional in a duo with any healer class. The Paladin already brings strong self-healing (heal spells + Lay on Hands), so adding a Cleric or Druid makes the character nearly unkillable. The Bard provides permanent mana regen for the Paladin's heals and the partner healer's spells.

  • With a Cleric: The Cleric handles main healing; the Paladin off-heals and keeps stuns up. Berserker DPS is uninterrupted. Bard mana regen keeps both healers' mana pools full.
  • With a Druid: Druid provides group heals + damage shields + ports. The Paladin can be more aggressive with Spellblade + stun, relying on the Druid for backup heals.
  • Berserker Frenzy + Paladin Smite = massive burst damage that no duo healer can match.
  • Ideal camps: Befallen (10-20), Mistmoore (20-30), Lower Guk (30-40), Sol B (40-50).

Duo with a Caster (Wizard or Enchanter)

The Paladin tanks while Bard mana regen fuels the caster's nukes. Berserker DPS + caster burst = extremely fast kills. The Enchanter can charm a pet for even more DPS.

  • With a Wizard: Paladin stun locks the target while Wizard nukes. Berserker Frenzy finishes. Bard Clarity song + Wizard meditate = zero downtime between pulls.
  • With an Enchanter: Enchanter haste + Bard haste stack for capped attack speed on the Berserker. Enchanter charm adds a fourth DPS source. Clarity song + Clarity spell stack for massive mana regen.
  • Bard pulling (lull + pacify) gives the caster partner a safe, clean pull every time.
  • Ideal camps: Oasis (10-20), Upper Guk (20-30), Sol A (30-40), City of Mist (40-50).

Full 3-Class Playstyle

When the combo is fully online (all three classes past level 10), the playstyle is fluid and powerful:

  1. Pre-fight: Bard activates Selo's, Anthem de Arms, and Clarity song. Paladin casts Valor (HP buff) and Holy Armor. Berserker activates War Cry and Berserker Strength.
  2. Pull: Bard uses lull/pacify to split packs. If that fails, Paladin tags with Flash of Light (blind + pull) or Berserker uses Furious Axe. Selo's run speed makes return trips fast.
  3. Engage: Paladin opens with Stun for initial aggro, then activates Spellblade (Strike loaded as proc). Berserker hits Frenzy immediately. Bard maintains song rotation.
  4. Sustain: Paladin cycles stuns and heals as needed. Berserker keeps Frenzy on cooldown and re-ups War Cry. Bard songs run passively — no twisting required in EQL.
  5. Burn phase: Berserker pops Berserker Rage (haste + crit) then Frenzy for maximum burst. Paladin uses Smite (3.5x damage, reduces LoH cooldown). Bard swaps to Warsong (haste) if not already active.
  6. Emergency: Paladin Lay on Hands for a full heal. Bard can use Brusco's Boastful Bellow (stun) for interrupt. Berserker Blood Rage converts incoming damage to damage bonus — use it when HP gets low to turn defense into offense.
  7. Between pulls: Bard Clarity song regens Paladin mana. Berserker needs no mana. Zero downtime.
