David — Paladin / Shaman / Necromancer

STR > WIS > STA — Plate melee hybrid with Shaman utility and Necromancer DoTs

Overview

David's combo is a melee hybrid with powerful support and damage-over-time capabilities. The Paladin provides plate armor, defensive stances, and heals. The Shaman adds slows, group buffs, and additional healing. The Necromancer contributes DoTs, a pet, and mana regen via Lich.

Unlike Dave (who uses Enchanter for crowd control), David uses Necromancer for sustained DPS and a pet tank. This combo is more self-sufficient but lacks strong crowd control.

Stat Priority

  1. STRENGTH — Increases melee damage (Primary weapon swing + kick)
  2. WISDOM — Increases mana pool (Paladin + Shaman spells)
  3. STAMINA — Hit points, survival
  4. CHA — Debuff/buff effectiveness (Shaman)

Key Abilities by Level

Paladin (plate tank, heals)

  • L1: Lay on Hands (self-heal), Light Healing
  • L9: Word of Health (group heal)
  • L15: Stun (interrupt), Divine Light (big heal)
  • L30: Group buffs (armor class, resists)
  • L45: High-level heals, stuns

Shaman (slows, buffs, DoTs)

  • L1: Minor Healing, Strength buff
  • L9: Tigir's Swarm (slow), Spirit of Wolf (run speed)
  • L15: Cannibalize (HP → Mana), Malo (MR debuff)
  • L24: Spirit of Eagle (group run speed), Befriend Animal
  • L32: Companion Spirit (summon spirit wolf pet)
  • L35: Tagar's Insects (top slow), Group HP buff
  • L45: Frenzied Spirit (upgraded pet), improved heals

Necromancer (DoTs, pet, utility)

  • L1: Darkness (snare), Disease Cloud
  • L4: Cavorting Bones (skeleton pet)
  • L9: Feign Death, Poison Bolt
  • L16: Lich (HP → Mana regen), improved pet
  • L20: Invoke Fear (fear), AoE disease
  • L29: Siphon Life (HP drain)
  • L39: Call to Grave (best classic pet)
  • L49: Invoke Death (max pet)

Equipment & Gear

David wears plate armor from Paladin, making him tanky. His BiS is detailed on the David's BiS page.

  • Primary: 1H weapon (dual wield)
  • Secondary: Off-hand 1H weapon (dual wield, NEVER shield)
  • ANY 1: Mithril Two-Handed Sword (31% haste)
  • ANY 2: Shield (AC + exaltation slots)

Group Synergy

  • Solo: Necromancer pet tanks → Paladin/DW melee → Shaman DoTs. Slow + pet keeps you alive.
  • Duo with Christy: David tanks, Christy heals + nukes. David's Shaman slows + Necro DoTs add DPS.
  • Group: Paladin tank + Shaman buffs + Necro DPS is a strong trio. Add a dedicated healer and DPS for full group.

Race Recommendations

  • Dark Elf (best for Necromancer synergy + DEX for procs)
  • Human (balanced stats, good for Paladin)
  • Ogre (best STR/STA for melee, stun immunity)
