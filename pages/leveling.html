Leveling Guide 1-50

Optimal leveling path for the EQL open beta (July 1-21).

🎯 Beta Goal

The pre-order beta runs July 1-21 with a wipe. Focus on: (1) learning your combo rotation, (2) testing charm/Lich/LoH mechanics, (3) finding ideal gear camps, (4) establishing duo synergy with your partner. XP rate in beta may differ from launch.

Leveling Mechanics

  • Effective level = lowest of your three class levels
  • All 16 classes level up to level 10; primary locks at 11
  • No hell levels, no race/class XP modifiers, no XP loss on death
  • XP cannot be turned off
  • Always meditating — no need to sit for mana regen

Leveling Strategy — No Backtrack (Recommended)

Pick your three classes at creation (primary), level 10 (third), and commit. Level straight to 50 in the same zones. Fastest path to endgame.

Drops: Enameled Black Chestplate (AC 25), Crown of the Froglok Kings (AC 12, FR+20, CR+20), Adamantite Band (HP+75), Ghoulbane components, Screaming Mace components. Named mobs on every floor. Rathe Mountains hill giants included as a money stop at 32-36.
45-48Plane of HateEntry at level 46. Planar gear — best in slot pre-Kunark. Imbrued Platemail set for Bards (CHA+9 per piece). Rod of Infinite Thought (Clarity clicky), Eye of Innoruuk (GCD reset + INT), Sprinkler of Suffering (Cleric nuke). Need group — up to 8 players in raid zones.
48-50Plane of FearCapstone pre-Kunark zone. Top-tier planar drops. Cazic-Thule encounter. Fear-heavy — Enchanter countermeasures valuable. Finish from 48 to 50 here. Level cap is 50.

💡 Money Stop

In the 32-36 range, take a brief detour to Rathe Mountains hill giants — best platinum/hour in classic. Fund your spells for all three combos. Hill giants are easy to pull and kill, drop platinum and gems worth 30-50pp each.

Duo Leveling Strategy

Dave (Bard/Pal/Enc) + Christy (Cle/Nec/Wiz) covers all roles perfectly:

  • Pull: Enchanter pacify or Bard lull → Paladin tanks → Cleric heals
  • CC: Enchanter mez adds → Necro fear/root as backup
  • DPS: Necro DoTs → Wizard nukes → Paladin stuns → Bard songs
  • Healing: Cleric main heals + Paladin off heals + Lay on Hands
  • Mana: Bard Clarity song + Enchanter Clarity spell + Necro Lich = infinite mana
  • Travel: Wizard ports + Selo's run speed = fastest travel in Norrath
  • Safety: Necro Feign Death + Wizard Evacuate + Cleric 96% Rez + Paladin Rez

💡 Pro Tip: Spell Vendors

Buy spells ahead of your level when you visit major cities. You can memorize them at any time — they only activate when you reach the required level. Don't waste trips! Some spells are also autogranted up to approximately level 10-20.

Open Beta Specific Advice

  • Account creation: Pre-order at everquestlegends.com to get beta access + name reservation
  • Client download: Check your account page for download links. Beta client may have separate install.
  • Character reservation: Reserve names for both characters early — popular names go fast
  • Join Discord: discord.gg/everquestlegends for bug reports, grouping, and tips
  • Beta feedback: Report bugs via the in-game system. Game Jawn is very responsive.
  • Server wipe: Everything wipes July 21. Don't hoard — experiment, explore, have fun.
