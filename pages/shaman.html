Shaman

The spirit-caller of the wilds — healer, slower, buffer, and pestilence dealer.

🌿 Shaman

Role: Healer / Slower / Buffer / DoT Dealer

Chain Armor2H BluntHealerUtility

Core Description

Shamans are spiritualists who draw power from the natural world and ancestral spirits. They are the premier slowing class in EverQuest, capable of reducing an enemy's attack speed by 75% through the iconic Tigir's Insects line. This makes them one of the most powerful and sought-after classes in any group.

Beyond slowing, Shamans bring a powerful suite of healing spells (second only to Clerics), stat buffs (Strength, Stamina, Agility), HP regeneration, and a devastating line of poison and disease-based Damage-over-Time spells. Their Spirit of Wolf provides a significant run speed buff to the entire group. The Malo line of spells lowers an enemy's magic and poison resistance, enabling their DoTs to land more reliably and supporting other casters in the group.

🌿 EQL Shaman Change

In EQL, Shaman heals have received significant boosts to compete more effectively with Clerics in the main-healer role. The slow line (Tigir's Insects) now has even more reliability and slightly reduced mana cost, making Shaman the undisputed king of mitigation. Spirit of Wolf is available at ~L14 (as in classic). Cannibalize retains its classic identity but has a slightly faster cast time, enabling better mana regen during downtime.

Stat Priorities

  1. Wisdom (WIS): Mana pool. Shaman spells are Wisdom-based and WIS directly determines how much mana you have for heals, slows, DoTs, and buffs.
  2. Stamina (STA): HP pool. Shamans use Cannibalize to convert HP into Mana, making a larger HP pool directly translate to more mana over time.
  3. Intelligence (INT): Secondary mana pool (less efficient than WIS for Shamans but still useful for meditating).
  4. Strength (STR): Carry capacity. Lowest priority as Shamans rarely rely on melee damage.

EQL-Specific Shaman Changes

  • Improved heals — Shaman healing has been buffed to better compete with Clerics as a primary healer
  • Tigir's Insects at ~L9 — slow line available early, same as classic progression
  • Spirit of Wolf at ~L14 — group movement speed increase available at the classic level
  • Malo line buffed — Malo/Mala/Malosini resistance debuffs are more effective in EQL, with improved landing rates
  • Cannibalize faster cast — reduced cast time makes HP-to-Mana conversion more efficient during combat
  • DoT damage increased — poison and disease DoTs have received damage buffs on EQL
  • All/all instruments — instrument focus items work for Shaman too in EQL
  • Slow is critical — Tigir's Insects line is the single most impactful mitigation tool in the game. Keep it memorized at all times.
  • Group utility — SoW makes Shaman essential for dungeon travel; Cannibalize makes them self-sufficient for mana

Shaman AA Priority

  1. Slow Efficiency — reduces mana cost and increases duration of slow spells
  2. Cannibalize Mastery — improves HP-to-Mana conversion ratio and reduces cast time
  3. Healing Gift — improves healing spell effectiveness and crit chance
  4. Spirit Call — increases duration and effectiveness of stat buffs
  5. DoT Focus — increases poison/disease DoT damage and duration
  6. Spirit of Wolf Mastery — increases run speed bonus and group radius
