Cleric + Necromancer + Wizard

Christy's Pure Caster / Healer / Damage Trifecta

Cleric Necromancer Wizard

🎯 Combo Rating

This is an A-Tier caster trio that excels at sustained damage and safety. Cleric provides unmatched healing + resurrection + buffs. Necromancer provides sustained DoT damage + pet utility + mana recovery (Lich). Wizard provides massive burst nuke damage + ports. The trio covers every caster role perfectly while the Bard/Paladin/Enchanter combo tanks.

Armor

Plate (Cleric)

Role

Healer + DoTs + Nukes

WIS > INT > STA

Difficulty

Medium-Low

Why This Combo Works

  • Cleric is the best healer in EverQuest. Plate armor for survivability. Complete heal replacement (Promise Renewal). New spell lines in EQL including the most new spells of any class. Resurrection. Essential buffs (Symbol, Valor). Can off-tank in emergencies.
  • Necromancer provides sustained DoT damage that stacks infinitely with Wizard nukes. Pet for extra DPS or tanking. Lich for mana regeneration. Fear, snare, root, lifetap. New DoT line exclusive to EQL. Feign Death for pulling safety.
  • Wizard provides massive burst damage. Highest DPS nukes in the game. Teleportation (ports) — critical for travel. Evacuation (group port out of danger). Damage shields.

🔗 Duo Synergy

This combo pairs with Dave's Bard+Paladin+Enchanter: you bring unmatched healing + sustained DoT + burst nuke. They bring tanking + crowd control + mana regen buffs. The full duo covers every group role.

💡 Recommended Starting Race

Cleric primary → Dwarf. 83 WIS gives you plenty of mana. High STR/STA for survivability (you'll wear plate). Necromancer secondary (Dark Elf bonuses for INT). Wizard third for ports. The plate armor from Cleric protects your otherwise-squishy caster body.

Stat Priority

  1. Wisdom (WIS): Primary mana stat for Cleric. Increases healing throughput.
  2. Intelligence (INT): Primary mana stat for Necro and Wizard. Increases nuke/DoT damage potential.
  3. Stamina (STA): Hit points. Cleric can wear plate but still benefits from HP.
  4. Dexterity (DEX): Reduces spell fizzle, affects weapon procs.
  5. Agility (AGI): Melee avoidance (less important for casters).

Playstyle Rotation

  1. Pre-fight: Cast Cleric buffs (Valor, Symbol). Necro Lich for mana. Wizard memorize ports.
  2. Pet management: Necro pet tanks secondary mob or adds DPS. In duo with Dave (using Paladin), pet can just DPS while Paladin tanks.
  3. DoT rotation: Necro applies Dots (Disease Cloud → highest DoT → tap). Stacks perfectly with Wizard nukes since damage types differ.
  4. Nuke burn: Wizard unloads full nuke rotation after Paladin establishes aggro. Let Dave (using Enchanter) cast Tashan first (lowers resists).
  5. Healing: Cleric keeps Promise Renewal HOT on Paladin. Use fast heals for spike damage. Superior Healing for emergencies.
  6. Mana management: Necro Lich + Bard's Clarity song + Enchanter's Clarity spell = massive regen. Cleric sits and meditates always.
  7. Emergency: Evacuate (Wiz) = zone-wide escape. Feign Death (Necro) for pulling safety. 96% Rez (Cleric) for post-wipe recovery.
  8. Travel: Wizard ports = fast travel anywhere. Combined with Bard Selo's run speed + Necro Feign Death scouting = fastest duo in Norrath.

Gearing Philosophy

Since Cleric provides plate armor, prioritize:

  • Wisdom gear for Cleric mana pool (healing throughput).
  • Intelligence gear for Necro/Wizard mana pools.
  • HP/AC — you'll survive better in plate.
  • Mana regeneration items dropped by mobs or found as quest rewards.
  • Focus effect items that boost healing or damage spell lines.
  • Two "any" slots for extra stat sticks or shields (full AC value from shield).

Synergies with Dave's Combo

  • Dave's Bard provides run speed (Selo's) — your Wizard doesn't need to port for short distances
  • Dave's Enchanter provides Clarity + Tashan (lowers resist) — your nukes/DoTs land harder
  • Dave's Paladin tanks — you never take aggro. Pure DPS + healing mode
  • Your Cleric rez + Dave (using Paladin) rez = double safety net
  • Your Wizard ports let the whole duo move anywhere
  • Your Necro DoTs + Wizard nukes + Dave (using Enchanter) charm pet = overwhelming damage
