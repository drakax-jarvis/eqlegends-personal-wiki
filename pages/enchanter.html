🧠 Enchanter

Role: Crowd Control / Buffer / Debuffer

Cloth ArmorDaggerCCMana Regen

Core Description

Enchanters are master illusionists and manipulators of the mind. They specialize in mesmerization, charming, runes, illusions, and mana regeneration. They are the premier crowd control class in EverQuest, able to lock down multiple enemies while keeping their group buffed and energized.

EQL-Specific Enchanter Changes

💪 EQL Enchanter — 2nd Most Popular Class in Beta

Enchanter charm is extremely viable in EQL. An AA makes charm 100% unbreakable. Valid charm targets exist in Planes of Hate, Fear, and Sky. Boon of the Guru (werewolf illusion + lifetap proc + dexterity) is confirmed an Enchanter ability. This makes Enchanter the second most popular class in beta.

Stat Priorities

  1. Intelligence (INT): Mana pool. Enchanter needs lots of mana for chain CC.
  2. Charisma (CHA): Affects charm tick checks. With unbreakable charm AA, less critical but still important.
  3. Stamina (STA): HP. Enchanters are cloth-wearers — every HP matters.
  4. Dexterity (DEX): Reduces spell fizzle.

Why Enchanter in Dave's Combo?

In the Bard/Paladin/Enchanter trio, the Enchanter provides the crowd control backbone. Bard handles passive buffs and pulling. Paladin tanks. Enchanter locks down adds, charms a DPS pet, and keeps Clarity + Haste active. The synergy of Bard's passive Clarity song + Enchanter's Clarity spell creates stacking mana regen.
