Bard

The master of song — jack of all trades, master of all.

🎵 Bard

Role: Support / Utility / Melee / Buffer

Plate Armor1H SlashSupportPull

Core Description

Bards are jack-of-all-trades adventurers who use songs to create a wide variety of magical effects. Though they often use melee weapons, bards are primarily a magic-using class, with the ability to wear plate armor and wield many different weapons and instruments.

Bards can do a little bit of everything. Their songs can regenerate health and mana, increase attack speed and accuracy, momentarily charm angry enemies, help friends resist many types of damage, and even directly damage enemies. Bards are perhaps best known for their ability to greatly increase the foot speed of their group.

🎵 EQL Bard Change

In EQL, Bard songs no longer require twisting! Songs activate once and sustain automatically. The classic EQ micromanagement of song rotation is gone. A confirmed AA allows Bards to autocast some songs. Instrument focus items are also all/all, making itemization much simpler.

Stat Priorities

  1. Charisma (CHA): Affects lull songs, charm tick checks. Bards have the most useful stats of any class but CHA is #1.
  2. Dexterity (DEX): Reduces song fizzle chance, affects weapon procs.
  3. Stamina (STA): HP. Bards wear plate so STA helps survivability.
  4. Strength (STR): Melee damage, carry capacity. Bard plate is heavy.
  5. Intelligence (INT): Only affects two songs that use mana (Solon's Bewitching Bravura and Denon's Desperate Dirge). Low priority for song-focused play.

EQL-Specific Bard Changes

  • No twisting — songs sustain automatically
  • Background Aura AA — Bards can maintain a non-targeted song as a passive background aura, enabling them to cast other class spells or melody other songs simultaneously. This is your most important Bard AA.
  • Autocast AA — new AA allows autocasting some songs
  • Bard AAs confirmed: Singing Mastery, Instrument Mastery, Jamfest, Song Range increase, and the Background Aura AA
  • All/all instruments — any class can use instrument focus items
  • /melody macro — use /stopcast then /melody with song slot numbers to cycle through songs automatically
  • Boon of the Guru (werewolf illusion + lifetap + dex) is an Enchanter ability, not Bard
  • Tracking — Bard has tracking ability (shared with Ranger, Druid)
  • Sneak — available to Bard, Ranger, Monk, Rogue

Bard AA Priority

  1. Background Aura — maintain a song while casting other spells. This is your #1 AA.
  2. Symphonic Aura — prevents stuns from breaking your song rotation
  3. Singing Mastery — improves song effectiveness
  4. Instrument Mastery — improves instrument modifier effectiveness
  5. Jamfest — enhanced song effects
  6. Song Range — increased song radius
